Aishah's Favourite Movies

Disclaimer: My tastes are not unique, but they are eclectic.

  1. The Room

    The Room is the best movie of all time. Tommy Wiseau is a genius from another planet, if not another dimension. I do not know what this movie is about and cannot explain it to you. I regularly quote this film because I will die if I do not.

  2. My Beautiful Laundrette

    My Beautiful Laundrette is a visually stunning movie about two men in a interracial relationship against the backdrop of an anti-immigrant English culture in a year I don't remember. I quite like the characters in this movie and their interactions.

  3. The Dora the Explorer Movie

    This movie has everything. Action. Suspense.
    Jungle puzzles.
    It is very important to not only myself, but also the Zeitgeist, the culture, and most people of importance around the world. I watched it twice in succession and would have watched it a third time if it wasn't 2:00am at that point.
